About InnoDAO

InnoDAO is a community-powered, Pi Network-based platform for driving real-world innovation. It aims to democratize innovation by applying DAO principles, funding and accelerating impactful projects in science, health, and sustainability. The platform uses decentralized tools, open collaboration, and AI to let pioneers propose, vote on, and build solutions together.

Built as a testnet prototype on the Pi Network, InnoDAO leverages the network’s Test-Pi environment for experimentation. Pi’s current Testnet phase encourages community-led development of decentralized applications, making it an ideal foundation for InnoDAO’s collaborative innovation model. Community members can help test the platform, provide feedback, and shape its evolution.
